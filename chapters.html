Founding Chapters

UniClean grows through city chapters led by local volunteers and partners.

Chapter 01 — Manzini, Eswatini

Coordinator: Mlandvo Mbhekwa Mkhweli

Focus: informal settlements, peri-urban fringes, and climate-displaced communities around Manzini. Youth networks like YCCIE, IYC, PAYP and South Sound Creates help spot and respond to pollution sites.

Chapter 02 — Ilorin, Nigeria

Coordinator: Jagun Quareeb Alabi

Focus: university campus waste, student-led action, and urban drain management in and around the University of Ilorin. UniClean works with student groups and community volunteers to turn reports into visible cleanups.
